About Michigan Fencing Solutions

Michigan Fencing Solutions is a locally owned and operated fencing company proudly serving homeowners and businesses throughout Michigan. We specialize in high-quality fencing solutions, including wood, vinyl, chain link, and decorative metal fencing tailored to meet your needs.

Our mission is simple: deliver durable, attractive fences with exceptional service. Whether you need a privacy fence for your backyard, a secure perimeter for your business, or custom fencing for a unique project, our team ensures expert installation and lasting results.

I’m Richard Sturd, founder of Michigan Fencing Solutions. We’re committed to quality workmanship, honest pricing, and complete customer satisfaction. Let us help you create the perfect fence for your property.
